Narendra Modi, in the early days of his political journey


Here's a time-line of Narendra Modi's life:


  • 1987-2001 - Modi's early years involved grassroots work with the RSS and organizational roles within the BJP, showcasing his dedication to the party.

  • 2001-2014 -Modi's tenure as Chief Minister of Gujarat marked a period of economic growth, but the 2002 Gujarat riots brought both challenges and criticism.

  • 2014 -Emerging as the BJP's candidate, Modi's dynamic campaign in the 2014 elections promised development, securing him the position of India's 14th Prime Minister.

  • 2014-2019 -Modi's first term focused on economic reforms, social welfare programs, and a decisive leadership style, reflecting in initiatives like "Swachh Bharat Abhiyan" and "Make in India."

  • 2016 - Modi's decision to demonetize high-denomination currency notes aimed at curbing corruption and promoting a digital economy, generating both praise and criticism.

  • 2019 -The resounding victory in the 2019 elections secured Modi a second term as Prime Minister, emphasizing popular support for his leadership.

  • 2020-2021 -Modi faced the unprecedented challenge of the COVID-19 pandemic, implementing lockdowns and relief measures to address the health crisis.

  • 2022-Current - Modi continues to lead India, addressing various domestic and international issues, showcasing resilience and adaptability in his leadership.
